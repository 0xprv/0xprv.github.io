## 🏆 DIF 24 Hackathon — 1st Place Winner

### [Decentralized PHC Credentials in the Future AI Era](https://devpost.com/software/test-7gtomq#updates)

**Duration:** Oct 2024 – Nov 2024  
**Tech Stack:** Rust · Python · JS/TS · ZK-SNARK

---

### 🚀 Overview

In this decentralized blockchain identity hackathon, I proposed a system for **PHC (Proof of Personhood Credentials)**.

The project leverages advanced cryptographic primitives and blockchain infrastructure to create a **secure, self-sovereign identity system**.

---

### 🔐 Core Idea

- User provides **thumbprint input**
- System generates a **cryptographic hash**
- Data structured using a **Merkle Tree**
- Stored securely and accessed via **identity wallet**
- Ensures:
  - Tamper resistance  
  - Privacy preservation  
  - Protection from identity theft  

---

### 🧠 Technical Highlights

- Zero-knowledge proof integration (**ZK-SNARKs**)
- Decentralized identity architecture
- Cryptographic verification without exposing raw biometric data
- Wallet-based identity access layer
